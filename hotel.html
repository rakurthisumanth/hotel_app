Spektrum Bengaluru Hotel

Spektrum at Bengaluru is the brand's flagship property, situated in a prestigious area with spectacular views of the surrounding area and the city skyline. The hotel offers a variety of rooms and suites to satisfy the demands of any guest, from budget-conscious to the most extravagant.

The Spektrum Bengaluru has something to offer everyone. With the airport so close to the hotel, guests will likely include schedule-adhering pilots, business travellers, vacationing families, or solo travellers indulging their creative streak. Located in a bustling metropolis, the hotel offers unparalleled hospitality, smart conference rooms, business-friendly services, easy access in and out of the city, and Instagrammable spaces. It is the perfect idyllic setting to watch airplanes land and take off.
