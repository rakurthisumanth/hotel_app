Room Description

Spektrum’s Galaxy Suite is the largest and most luxurious of our rooms, and designed to offer our guests an out-of-the-world experience, in every sense. This luxury futuristic style room comes equipped with state of the art equipment and sleek metallic accents. The bedroom is separated from the living area by a sliding door, featuring a plush king-size bed, luxury bedding, and plenty of closet space.

The bathroom is a dream. Luxury amenities and fluffy towels are standard in the spa-like bathroom, which also features a deep soaking tub and a separate walk-in shower.

A variety of high-tech luxuries, such as a giant flat-screen TV, a sound system, and a home automation system that allows visitors to manage various components of the room with the touch of a button, may also be included in "The Galaxy" suite. A private balcony or terrace with a spectacular view of the neighborhood gives this a quaint yet worldly effect
