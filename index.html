About Spektrum

Hotels & Resorts

Everyone is a part of the Spektrum. Our community extends, reaches, and helps everyone identify with who they are.

At Spektrum, we believe that creating exceptional spaces is more than just a job, “it’s a passion”. Our philosophy is to approach every project with a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, and to create spaces that improve and amplify the lives of people.

With an emphasis on individualized services, cutting-edge design, and eco-friendliness, our brand concept is to deliver a smooth experience for our customers. We think that a hotel’s atmosphere, service, and location are just as important as the quality of the room itself when it comes to creating a memorable stay.

Spektrum—where every element finds its place, and every guest finds their home.

Spektrum Business

Spektrum Business is a more extensive, dynamic setup catering to fast-paced business people coming into town from different cities or countries.

Located in convenient spots across vibrant cities in India, the Spektrum Hotel offers some much-needed rest to these globetrotters, combined with unparalleled hospitality. The ambiance and service will help them regain their mindset ready to take on another adventurous day.

Spektrum Leisure

Spektrum Grand is our leisure segment which is not just a hotel—it's an identity curated for those who enjoy indulging in the finer things in life. They cater to premium experiences across demographics and themes.

It is a perfectly idyllic setting with stunning views. The hotel offers plenty of services such as spas, swimming pools, conference rooms, and more.
